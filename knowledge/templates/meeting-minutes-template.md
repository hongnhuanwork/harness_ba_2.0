# Meeting Minutes Template

## Purpose
Captures agendas, attendees, discussions, decisions, and action items from alignment sessions.

## Traceability
Traces back to: Stakeholder Register. Traces forward to: Decision Record, Task list.

## Metadata
- Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- Time: [HH:MM]
- Meeting Title: [Title]
- Facilitator: [Name]
- Attendees: [Names]
- Absentees: [Names]

## Required Sections
## 1. Meeting Objectives
List the target goals of the meeting.

## 2. Discussion Summary
Summarize the key discussion points, debates, and inputs.

## 3. Key Decisions Made
- Decision 1: [Details, links to ADR if created]

## 4. Action Items & Next Steps
| Task Description | Owner | Due Date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Details] | [Name] | [YYYY-MM-DD] | [Open/Closed] |

## Optional Sections
## 5. Next Agenda Items
List topics scheduled for subsequent alignments.

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] Action items have specific owners and dates.
- [ ] Decisions are logged clearly.
- [ ] Sent to all attendees post-meeting.

# Risk Register Template

## Purpose
Identifies, prioritizes, and logs project and product risks, assigning mitigation plans.

## Traceability
Traces back to: Constraints, Assumptions. Traces forward to: PMO Health Checks, Release Readiness reviews.

## Metadata
- Project ID: [ID]
- Register Owner: [Name]
- Last Updated: [YYYY-MM-DD]

## Required Sections
## 1. Risk Matrix & Prioritization
Define the criteria for evaluating risk Likelihood (1-5) and Impact (1-5). Risk Score = Likelihood x Impact.

## Optional Sections
## 3. Contingency Triggers
Define specific indicators that trigger backup plans or budget escalations.

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] Every risk has a description of its cause and its consequence.
- [ ] Mitigation strategies are actionable and scheduled.
- [ ] All risks with a score > 12 have assigned owners.

# 🚀 Initiating a New Project

## Purpose

Walk the user through describing their project idea, highlighting the critical dimensions required for analysis.

## Output Message

To kick off a new project, please describe your project idea below. To help me understand it without immediately triggering a clarification loop, try to provide details on:

1. **Domain**: What industry or business field does this project belong to? (e.g., FinTech, E-commerce, Healthcare)
2. **Stakeholders**: Who are the primary users, sponsors, and key actors involved?
3. **Scope**: What are the main features in-scope, and what is explicitly out-of-scope?
4. **Objectives**: What are the business goals, problems to solve, or target KPIs?

---

## Action

Please describe your project idea in detail in your next reply. 

*Note: Once you submit your idea, I will route you to the **Intake Gate** to assess input completeness, load templates, and prepare your project workspace.*

# Purpose

To ensure that software decision logic matches business policies and compliance mandates.

# When To Use

- Business rule designs completed
- Audit readiness reviews
- Policy change alignments

# Inputs

- Business Rules Catalog
- Compliance parameters
- Permutation results

# Process

1. Present decision tables and optimized formulas to Policy Owners.
2. Validate rule boundaries against compliance and legal documents.
3. Obtain sign-off for rule logic.
4. Log validated rules in the repository.
5. Draft Business Rules Validation Report.

# Output

- Business Rules Validation Report

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Formulas are signed off by financial/legal representatives.
- [ ] Decision paths conform to compliance mandates.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Policy Owner and Legal Counsel must sign off.

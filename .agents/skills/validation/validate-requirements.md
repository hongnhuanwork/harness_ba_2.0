# Purpose

To obtain formal stakeholder consensus and sign-off on requirements before design starts.

# When To Use

- Requirements phase active
- BRD/FRD completion
- Pre-design alignment meetings

# Inputs

- BRD/FRD drafts
- Traceability matrix
- Stakeholder Register

# Process

1. Conduct requirements walkthrough sessions with business stakeholders.
2. Map requirements back to business objectives.
3. Document agreed-upon updates or adjustments.
4. Facilitate resolution of conflicting expectations.
5. Archive formal sign-off.

# Output

- Requirements Validation Report
- Signed Validation Agreement

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Sponsors and business team representatives attend walkthroughs.
- [ ] Signed confirmation is documented.
- [ ] Any adjustments are logged.

# Human Review Required

Yes. All primary stakeholders must sign off.

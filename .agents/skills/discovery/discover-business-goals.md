# Purpose

To define clear, measurable business objectives that align all technical design and development decisions with organizational value.

# When To Use

- Initial requirements gathering phase begins
- Scope alignment meetings are initiated
- Strategic direction changes are announced

# Inputs

- Executive briefings and SOW
- Business cases or vision documents
- Interview transcripts with business sponsors

# Process

1. Extract strategic statements and problem statements from input documents.
2. Analyze business pain points and convert them into target objectives.
3. Format objectives using the SMART framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
4. Define primary success metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for the overall project.
5. Map business goals to high-level system capabilities.

# Output

- Business Objectives List
- SMART Goal Mapping Matrix

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Every goal is defined with at least one measurable KPI.
- [ ] Goals align with the project scope documented in AGENT.md.
- [ ] No technical implementation details are present in the business goals.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Executive Sponsor or Product Owner must sign off on business goals and success metrics.

# Purpose

To discover potential high-value features, automation solutions, or integrations that can enhance system utility.

# When To Use

- Market analysis or technology reviews are conducted
- Pain points analysis highlights optimization paths
- Ideation and brainstorming sessions occur

# Inputs

- Pain Points Register
- Industry benchmarks and technology trends documentation
- Competitor analysis reports

# Process

1. Analyze current pain points and look for technological or procedural shortcuts.
2. Brainstorm automation paths, self-service portals, or integration links.
3. Evaluate feasibility, potential return on investment (ROI), and alignment with business objectives.
4. Draft opportunity concepts with high-level benefits statements.
5. Submit concepts to stakeholders for prioritization.

# Output

- Opportunities Analysis Matrix
- Proposed Value Propositions

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Opportunities directly address mapped pain points or business goals.
- [ ] Feasibility estimates are realistic based on current project constraints.
- [ ] Opportunities specify target benefits and success criteria.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Product Owner or Business Analyst Lead must approve the opportunities before inclusion in product roadmap.

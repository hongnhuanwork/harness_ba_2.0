# Purpose

To establish objective, quantifiable metrics for measuring the success and performance of the delivered solution.

# When To Use

- Business Goals Discovery is completed
- Performance monitoring setup is required
- UAT planning starts

# Inputs

- Business Goals List
- As-Is Process documentation
- SLAs (Service Level Agreements)

# Process

1. Review business goals and objectives.
2. Define specific indicators that demonstrate achievement of goals (e.g., page load time, transaction volume, conversion rate).
3. Establish baseline metrics from current systems.
4. Define target thresholds (minimum, target, stretch goals).
5. Document reporting frequency and data sources for tracking KPIs.
6. Consolidate in a KPI Definition Register.

# Output

- KPI Definition Register
- Measurement Strategy Outline

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Every KPI is measurable and linked to a data source.
- [ ] KPIs are realistic and directly mapped to business goals.
- [ ] Calculation formulas are documented.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Business Sponsor or PO must sign off on KPIs and success targets.

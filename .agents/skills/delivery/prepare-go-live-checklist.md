# Purpose

To verify that all operational, support, and business systems are ready to run the new system in production.

# When To Use

- Go-live date scheduled
- Deployment planning starts
- Operational readiness meetings

# Inputs

- Deployment Checklist
- Support desk staffing logs
- Client communication templates

# Process

1. Draft checklist of business and operational validation checks.
2. Assign support team schedules for hypercare period.
3. Draft communication templates for users ('system maintenance notice').
4. Outline escalation contact details.
5. Compile into Go-Live checklist.

# Output

- Go-Live Operational Checklist
- Escalation Plan & Roster

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Hypercare schedules cover at least 2 weeks post-launch.
- [ ] Contact phone numbers and email paths are functional.
- [ ] Communications are drafted and approved.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Project Manager and Product Owner must sign off.

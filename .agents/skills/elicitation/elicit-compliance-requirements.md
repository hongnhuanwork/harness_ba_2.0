# Purpose

To ensure the system conforms to national, international, and industry regulations and laws.

# When To Use

- Project initialization
- Legal or security audits
- Entry into new geographic markets

# Inputs

- Regulatory guidelines (e.g. GDPR, CCPA, PCI-DSS)
- Corporate legal policies
- Industry compliance manuals

# Process

1. Identify regulatory frameworks applicable to the system.
2. Elicit explicit rules regarding data residency, consent management, audit trails, and accessibility.
3. Draft requirements mapping rules to specific legal sections.
4. Document accessibility targets (e.g. WCAG 2.1 AA).
5. Review with the legal and security teams.

# Output

- Compliance Requirements Specification
- Regulatory Mapping Matrix

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] Each requirement points directly to a regulatory citation.
- [ ] Auditing and logging requirements are explicitly defined.
- [ ] Consent and data erasure workflows are detailed.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Legal Counsel or Compliance Officer must sign off.

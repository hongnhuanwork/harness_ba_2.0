# Purpose

To ensure all documented business policies, algorithms, and validation rules are logically sound.

# When To Use

- Business Rules Catalog updates
- Logic errors detected during development
- Policy audits

# Inputs

- Business Rules Catalog
- Policy documents
- Rule exception requests

# Process

1. Compare catalog rules against source policy manuals.
2. Check logical formulas for mathematical correctness.
3. Look for conflicting conditional pathways.
4. Ensure rules are structured clearly for business and developer readability.
5. Draft Business Rules Audit report.

# Output

- Business Rules Audit Report

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] No logic deadlocks exist.
- [ ] All variables are defined in the glossary.
- [ ] Verification is aligned with corporate compliance.

# Human Review Required

Yes. Policy Owner or Business Analyst Lead must approve updates.

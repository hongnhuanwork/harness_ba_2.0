# Purpose

To audit the raw requirements backlog, identifying gaps or conflicts before drafting formal specifications.

# When To Use

- Elicitation phase finishes
- Backlog cleanup is requested
- Requirements phase transition

# Inputs

- Raw Requirements Log
- Scope baseline
- Business objectives list

# Process

1. Scan requirements for duplicates or redundant definitions.
2. Identify conflicting requirements (e.g., 'system must save history' vs 'system must delete history immediately').
3. Check for vague qualifiers ('fast loading', 'simple UI').
4. Flag requirements that fall outside the scope baseline.
5. Compile Requirements Audit Log.

# Output

- Requirements Audit Log

# Quality Checklist

- [ ] All requirements have assigned owners and categories.
- [ ] Vague requirements are flagged with suggested clarifications.
- [ ] Out-of-scope requirements are highlighted.

# Human Review Required

No. Can be reviewed and resolved during planning sessions with the PO.
